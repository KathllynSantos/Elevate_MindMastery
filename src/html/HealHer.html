Introduction

Welcome to your course HealHer. This course is divided into 10 modules.

Module 1

Inner Child Healing & Shadow Work is a transformative journey of self-awareness and emotional healing. By recognizing childhood wounds through mindfulness and journaling, we uncover deep-seated fears and patterns. Communicating with your inner child and reparenting yourself fosters self-love and resilience. Daily self-care and emotional awareness strengthen healing. Embracing this process leads to authenticity, empowerment, and a fulfilling life.

Module 2

In this module, we will take a closer look at two attachment theories and compare how they are similar and how they are different. The first section of Module 2 will discuss the attachment theory based on Bowlby’s research, whilst the second half will look at the results of the study performed by Schaffer and Emerson.
