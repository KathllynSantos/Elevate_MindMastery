Attachment Theory

Diploma Course

A massive part of the human experience is the relationships we form, the emotional bonds we create, and the attachments we cultivate. Have you ever stepped back and wondered why we behave in the ways we do around others? Why do some thrive in relationships that others would struggle with?

This course explores how we form attachments and how these affect us through infancy, childhood, and adulthood. We’ll learn about the stages and specific types of attachment, the attachment styles of children and adults, and the limitations and practical uses of attachment theory.
